Endangered species

It was widespread destruction of African nature that inspired the English biologist, Sir Julian Huxley, to found the World Wide Fund for Nature (WWF) in 1961.

We carry out active environmental work in the field, but also behind desks, in collaboration with other players, in the corridors of politics and at international negotiating tables.
